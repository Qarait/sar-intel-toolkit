# Validation

## Real footage test

Input:
- 3840×2160 street footage
- 25 fps
- ~14 seconds

Results:
- 1699 frame-level alerts
- 29 confirmed tracks
- ~58.6× reduction from raw detections to confirmed tracks

Notes:
- This is frame-level detection and tracking validation.
- It is not operational SAR validation.
- Geotagging remains approximate.
